More About Me

I am a recent graduate with a joint honors degree in Economics and Business Information Systems, a minor in Applied Statistics and a certificate in Business Analytics from the University of Pittsburgh. I have a passion in using business, analytics, and data science to drive social and environmental change.

As an Honors Research Hub Fellow at the Center for Sustainable Business (CSB), I completed a data engineering project to create the necessary data pipelines to effectively record, analyze, and track the impact of the CSB's various operations. I also instructed Pitt Business and local community members on Excel and LinkedIn skills as a Student Trainer for the Functional Literacy Program, after completing certifications for both platforms. Earlier in my college career, I was part of the inaugural cohort of the Social Equity Honors Research Hub at the Frederick Honors College, where I designed an independent research project to examine social policy using reinforcement learning frameworks in R.

I have analytical, leadership, and communication skills and my goal is to leverage my education and experience to contribute to the advancement of sustainability and social justice in the business world.
